Property Pricing:

Single housing (S) - For single housing, base price is $1000 a month. Price changes based on number of bedrooms, bathrooms, garages, and square footage, including the yards. The lease lasts one year then month to month.

Apartment Complexes(A) - Apartment complexes start at $500 dollars a month, then change based on bedrooms, bathrooms, garages, and square footage of the apartment. Lease length is as follows: 3,6,12 months. After this, the rent is month to month.

Short-term vacation rental (V) - Based on the type of home and bedrooms and bathrooms, the price will vary. The leases will last days, weeks, or months. However, weekly leases come with a 25% up-charge, and daily come with a 40% up charge.
